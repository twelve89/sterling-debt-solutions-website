Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Sterling Debt Solutions is committed to providing a website and consumer account portal that are accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of ability or technology. We believe that every consumer should be able to review their account information, make payments, and communicate with us in a way that works for them.

Our Commitment

We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities, including users of assistive technologies such as screen readers, screen magnifiers, and alternative input devices.

Accessibility Features

Our website and consumer portal include the following accessibility features:

  • Keyboard navigation. All interactive elements can be accessed using a keyboard.
  • Semantic HTML. Pages use headings, landmarks, and ARIA attributes to convey structure to assistive technologies.
  • Text alternatives. Meaningful images include alternative text descriptions.
  • Color contrast. Text and background color combinations are chosen to meet or exceed WCAG 2.1 AA contrast requirements.
  • Responsive design. Our site adapts to different screen sizes and zoom levels.
  • Reduced motion. Animations respect the user's operating system preference for reduced motion.
  • Clear forms. Form fields have visible labels and accessible error messages.

Ongoing Efforts

Accessibility is an ongoing process. We periodically review our website and consumer portal, test with assistive technologies, and work with our team and outside experts to identify and address accessibility barriers. As new content and features are added, we work to ensure they meet our accessibility standards.

Third-Party Content

Some portions of our website may include content, services, or functionality provided by third parties (for example, embedded maps, payment processors, or analytics tools). While we work with vendors who are committed to accessibility, we do not control the accessibility of third-party content.

Feedback and Assistance

Need assistance or want to report an accessibility issue?
If you experience difficulty accessing any part of our website or consumer portal, or if you need information or services in an alternative format, please contact us. We will work with you to provide the information, item, or transaction you seek through an accessible alternative.
